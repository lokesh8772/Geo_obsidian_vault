1. simple fold - symmetrical fold
2. complex fold - 
	1. asymmetrical 
	2. overfold
	3. recumbant(highly overfolded but continuous)
	4. overthrust(folding disconnects ....)
#### faults - 
1. normal faults -> divergent 
2. thrust faults - convergent
3. transform faults - transverse boundaries
